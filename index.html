Kuala Heaven

Kuala Lumpor is the capital of Malysia. If I say a piece of heaven has fallen down in that piece of land, you might say I'm exaggerating!

In my first visit to Kuala, I was on my honeymoon, when we reached the hotel, unpacked some of our luggage, I told her to go take a quick walk to see where we are!

I literally couldn't believe my eyes! Is that really true?! Those people are friendly that I think they are angels!, Those little green grass spots at each tile joint!, and those clean streets!

We ended up touring all around the neighbourhoods around ours and we also visited many places on that very first day

  • The Muzium Telekom, which is the communications museum.
  • The twin towers or Petronas towers.
  • Cathedral of St. John the Evangelist
